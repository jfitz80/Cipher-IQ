Cipher IQ

Crack the code. Train your logic.

Cipher IQ is a number-code logic puzzle game where players crack hidden ciphers using deduction, limited attempts, daily streaks, and feedback clues.

How to Play

Guess the hidden number code before your attempts run out. After each guess, Cipher IQ gives you feedback to help you determine which digits are exact matches and which digits are correct but misplaced.

Daily Cipher

A new Daily Cipher resets each day. Crack the code, build your streak, earn points, and keep your logic sharp.

Privacy

Cipher IQ is designed as a simple puzzle experience. Game progress may be stored locally on your device, including score, streaks, badges, and daily challenge status.
