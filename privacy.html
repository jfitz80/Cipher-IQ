Cipher IQ Privacy Policy Effective Date: June 1, 2026 Cipher IQ is a number-code logic puzzle game. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. Cipher IQ may store game progress locally on your device, including score, streaks, badges, daily challenge status, and app preferences. This information stays on your device and is used only to support gameplay. If you enable daily reminder notifications, Cipher IQ uses your device’s local notification system to send reminders. Notification preferences are managed on your device and can be changed in iOS Settings. Cipher IQ does not use third-party advertising trackers, does not sell user data, and does not require account registration.
